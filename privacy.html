Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Data Autonomy

We respect your privacy. BeatMaster is built with the principle of minimal data collection. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. Third-Party Integrations

Apple Music & iTunes API

Our application utilizes the Apple Music and iTunes Search API to provide high-quality music previews and cover art. Interaction with these services is governed by Apple's own privacy policies. We do not store your Apple Music library data or credentials on our servers.

Google AdMob

To keep the application free for everyone, we display advertisements via Google AdMob. Google may use advertising identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can manage your ad preferences through your Android device settings.

Firebase Cloud Firestore

Our real-time multiplayer lobbies are powered by Firebase. We generate anonymous session IDs to synchronize game states between players. No permanent personally identifiable information (PII) is required to play.
